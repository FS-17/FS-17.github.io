SPL's Internal Event: Values Challenge

2024-12-25
Web DevelopmentInteractive ExperienceEvent Technology

Developed an interactive experience for SPL's internal event, featuring seven challenges aligned with the company's core values. Employees engaged in various activities, including puzzle-solving, drawing, scenario-based problem-solving, and a collaborative game, to reinforce their understanding and commitment to SPL's principles.

Features & Functionality

Puzzle Challenge

A timed puzzle game where employees assemble a picture representing "We move fast."

Drawing Challenge

Employees draw an image representing "We commit to doing more" in three rounds, with the option to improve their drawing in each round.

Scenario-Based Challenge

Employees respond to scenarios related to "We exceed our customers' expectations," with AI evaluating their solutions.

Collaborative Game

Two players work together, one with instructions and the other interacting with the screen, to complete a task representing "We cooperate and share together."

Confession Booth

A platform for employees to anonymously share their thoughts and experiences, representing "We are characterized by trust and transparency."

Idea Submission

Employees combine elements related to SPL services and innovative technologies to create and submit new ideas, representing "We innovate solutions for the future."

Interactive Challenges

Employees solve various challenges related to SPL's services and values, representing "We always overcome challenges."

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Tight Development Timeline: The project required rapid development and testing to meet the event deadline.

Solution: Utilized agile development methodologies, including rapid prototyping, iterative development, and close communication with the client, to ensure timely delivery.

Challenge: Ensuring Seamless Integration of Diverse Challenges: The project involved creating seven different interactive experiences, each with unique technical requirements.

Solution: Employed a modular design approach and leveraged JavaScript libraries and frameworks to streamline development and ensure seamless integration between the different challenges.

Challenge: Optimizing for Touch Screen Interaction: The event primarily utilized touch screen displays, requiring careful consideration of user interface and user experience design.

Solution: Conducted thorough testing on touch screen devices and implemented responsive design principles to ensure optimal usability and a smooth interactive experience.
